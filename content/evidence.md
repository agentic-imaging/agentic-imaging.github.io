# Evidence

The public evidence for AgX is produced by a preregistered, sealed, label-blind protocol:
development and confirmatory data are separated; protocols, budgets, seeds, and stop rules are
cryptographically sealed before held-out labels are revealed; and scoring happens once, after an
authorized reveal, with the statistics preregistered. Until that reveal completes and a human
release gate signs off, **this site shows no real evidence numbers**.

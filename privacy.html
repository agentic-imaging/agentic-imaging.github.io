Privacy

This site is designed to know nothing about you.

  • No analytics, no trackers, no beacons. There is no measurement of visits, clicks, or reading behavior of any kind.
  • No cookies. The site sets no cookies at all.
  • No external requests. All fonts are system fonts; all scripts, styles, and images are served from this site itself. Your browser contacts no third party by loading these pages.
  • Local theme preference. If you use the light/dark toggle, your choice is stored in your own browser's localStorage under a single key (agx-theme). It never leaves your device and you can clear it from your browser at any time.
  • Hosting. The site is a set of static files. The host that serves them (e.g., GitHub Pages) may keep standard server logs under its own privacy policy; the site itself adds nothing to that.
